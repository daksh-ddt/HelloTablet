What is HelloTablet?


HelloTablet is an app that can answer polar (yes-or-no) questions in addition to the factoid questions that are more commonly answered by Google Now, Siri, and others.

The app demonstrates one use of a system for predicting entailment that I designed as part of my Master's thesis at New York University.

How Does it Work?


HelloTablet retrieves sentences from the web that are similar to the user's question.

It predicts whether or not each of these sentences entails the question. HelloTablet then combines these predictions to return a final answers.

What is Entailment?


Entailment is the relationship between sentences that holds when one sentence follows from another. For example,

"I ate food" follows from "I ate pizza".

"Someone passed the test" follows from "Everyone passed the test".

"Nobody ate" does not follow from "Someone ate".
